Overview

Click is Adidas's global B2B e-commerce platform used by wholesale customers worldwide. I led two major initiatives: the wholesale mobile app from research through global launch in 15+ markets, and co-created the Click design system that accelerated the entire team by 60%.

The platform serves thousands of retail employees globally, enabling them to order products directly from the sales floor through barcode scanning, access order history for one-tap reordering, and work offline when connectivity is poor.

Web Platform

Global B2B e-commerce platform serving wholesale customers across 15+ markets with comprehensive product catalog, ordering, and account management.

Restock App

Led the wholesale mobile app from research through launch in 15+ markets. Designed a scanning-based reordering system that made ordering 40% faster for retail employees on the sales floor.

Click Design System

Co-built the Click design system from scratch using Atomic Design principles. The system includes 50+ documented components, design tokens, and usage guidelines—accelerating design-to-development time by 60%.

Personalization

Designed personalized experiences based on buyer behavior, order history, and product preferences—helping retail employees find relevant products faster.

Impact

The Adidas Click platform and mobile app now serve thousands of wholesale buyers globally, while the Click design system has been adopted as the standard across all Adidas B2B products.
