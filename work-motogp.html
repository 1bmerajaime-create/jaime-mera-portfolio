Project Overview

MotoGP partnered with Accenture to modernize its digital presence and explore new feature concepts. I created visual guidelines, designed proof-of-concept experiences, and supported a content-first direction.

Challenge & Impact

The platform needed to feel fast, premium, and mobile-first while supporting real-time racing data and video. The new visual system improved content hierarchy, discoverability, and fan engagement.
