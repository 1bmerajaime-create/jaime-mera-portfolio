Project Overview

BeeData is a B2B database service that uses AI to help businesses extract value from their data. I reframed the experience as a business intelligence advisor, making complex analytics accessible to non-technical users.

Challenge & Impact

Adoption was low because the interface was engineered for technical users. The redesign introduced progressive disclosure, contextual guidance, and AI-powered insights. Results included a 20-30% subscription increase, reduced onboarding time, and higher NPS.
