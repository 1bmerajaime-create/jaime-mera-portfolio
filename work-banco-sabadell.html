Project Overview

Banco Sabadell partnered with Accenture to create a new responsive commercial site for retail banking, evolving into a full digital brand transformation that unified multiple segments. I helped define the visual language, personas, and mobile-first experiences.

Challenge & Impact

The bank needed a modern, cohesive digital strategy that improved lead generation and scaled across segments. The Galatea design system was built from scratch to ensure consistency and speed, improving mobile conversion rates and reducing design-to-development time.
